Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide quality information on a wide variety of health topics. It is going to serve as a means for people to take charge of their health through intentional action.

Audience

The website is built with the idea to provide to an audience who are looking at improving their wellbeing. Also, to those who want to satisfy their curiosity about certain health topics.
